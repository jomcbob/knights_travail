Knights Travail

♞ The Knight in Chess

  • Movement: L-shape — two squares in one direction, one square perpendicular.
  • Jumping: Can leap over any piece — super unique!
  • Forks: Great at attacking two or more pieces at once.
  • Short-range: But powerful in closed, tactical positions.
  • Center control: Best when placed near the center of the board.
  • Corner weakness: In corners, it controls only 2 squares.
  • Notation: Written as "N" in chess moves(the King is K).
